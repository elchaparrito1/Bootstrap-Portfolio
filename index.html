About Me

Hello. Welcome to my website. I am passionate about putting hard work and dedication into everything that I do. What drives me is the challenge of the task. This is evident in the practice that I put into playing and excelling at sports in my youth. As I grew older, I accepted new challenges such as volunteering and working for my church, and learning new languages, customs, and cultures along the way.

It was my love for learning Spanish and Portuguese that led me to study history at the University of Utah. The challenge of academia taught me self-determination, and how to look at the bigger picture; how to problem solve, and think critically.

I am a man of many passions who believes in refining one's craft. Whether its learning how to become a better father and husband, preparing the best dish of Mexican mole, or discovering what techniques work best for training my horses, I'm always up to the challenge.

This is what excites me about the challenge of learning to code and become a computer programmer. It will test all my skills, and push me to new limits, and opportunities that I hope to attain in life through hard work and determination.
